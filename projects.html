Research

Kawasak.AI

Inspired by my younger sister who had Kawasaki disease, I started developing the first AI image recognition algorithm for Kawasaki disease early diagnosis at 14. Currently, I'm researching alongside leading doctors at the UCSD Kawasaki Disease Research Center/Rady Children's Hospital.

Some of my experiences include crowdsourcing the first image database for KD research, conducting end-to-end development of novel deep learning models, and presenting at conferences such as Regeneron ISEF, Scripps Research, World Summit AI, and International KD Symposium!

Cybersecurity

CyberPatriot XIII National Champion

Since I was young, I've loved taking apart computers and understanding tech from a fundamental level, leading me to discover interests in cybersecurity and ethical hacking :) Previously, I taught defensive cybersecurity and created virtual images (VMWare) for 120+ students. My team and I were also crowned CyberPatriot National Champions in 2021 by the US Air Force Association.

Certified in CompTIA IT Fundamentals+, GIAC Foundational Cybersecurity Technologies (SEC275), GIAC Penetration Tester (GPEN).

Nonprofit

Zipline Theory 501(c)(3)

I lead initiatives at Zipline Theory for a global community of 5,000+ learners, teaching young people how to solve the world's difficult problems beyond the conventional curriculum.

Most notably, we hosted a 3-week cybersecurity program for girls of military families in accordance with the U.S. Department of Defense, with funding from the National Center for Women in Technology, Apple, Google, Northrop Grumman, Amazon, Walmart, the Televisa Foundation, and the Cognizant USA Foundation.

Fencing

Competitive saber fencer

I love fencing (aka "physical chess") and fence on the national and international level! One of my favorite experiences was representing the USA at the Romania World Cup (U17 European Circuit), where I placed 5th place individually out of 200 fencers from 25 countries.

Some other fencing highlights include being captain of Junior Olympic Silver medal team, getting Top 30 in Division I at December North American Cup, and winning Junior Champion at UCSD Bladerunner.

Podcast

Delta X Podcast

I host a podcast with 500+ downloads/episode and write a newsletter breaking down technology, startups, and innovation. Previous guests include the creator of the 1st COVID-19 website, Harvard Business School associate professors, and founder of the largest community of student hackers/builders.

Featured at RSA Conference, channel coverage of the largest cybersecurity conferences including DEFCON and BlackHat.

Writing

Creative Writer

In 4th grade, I was encouraged to submit a short story to a state writing competition, which won 1st place in California and was recognized by Assemblymember Brian Maienschein.

Since then, I've written a personal narrative piece published in the New York Times (selected out of 11,000 essays by NYT journalists), and served as President and Editor-in-Chief of The Featheralist, an award-winning Columbia Press Association Gold literary publication. Always open to book/poetry reccomendations! :)
